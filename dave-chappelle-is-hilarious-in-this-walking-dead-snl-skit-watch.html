Dave Chappelle Is Hilarious in This Walking Dead SNL Skit: Watch

They’re back! Dave Chappelle hilariously revived all of his most popular characters during a Walking Dead skit on Saturday Night Live on Saturday, November 12.

The 43-year-old comedian introduced the clip by professing his love for the hit AMC zombie series, and his shock at the dual deaths of characters Glenn (Steven Yeun) and Abraham (Michael Cudlitz) earlier this year.

The five-minute clip kicks off with Chappelle dressed as Negan (Jeffrey Dean Morgan), complete with black leather jacket, red scarf and barbed wire-wrapped baseball bat.

“I just can’t decide which one of you is going to dance with death tonight,” he says as he saunters down a line of kneeling men. “Well, I’ve got an idea. Bubblegum, bubblegum in a dish. How many pieces do you wish?”

“Bubblegum?” Chapelle as Tyrone Biggums says, scratching his neck. “I’d like two, please. My mouth feels dry.”

“Ah, a feisty one,” Chappelle’s Negan says. “I like that.”

“Well, with all due respect, Mr. Negan, in my line of work when you’re on your knees, they don’t give you bubblegum,” Biggums says. “They give you penis.”

As Chappelle-as-Negan makes his way down the line, he encounters other fan favorites such as the Time Haters, Lil Jon and Clayton Bigsby.
