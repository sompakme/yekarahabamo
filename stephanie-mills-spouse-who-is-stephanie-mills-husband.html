Stephanie Mills Spouse: Who Is Stephanie Mills Husband?

Stephanie Dorthea Mills was born on March 22, 1957, in Bedford–Stuyvesant, Brooklyn, New York, the U.S. Stephanie Mills is an American singer and songwriter who became well known as “Dorothy” in the original 7 Time Tony Award Winning Broadway run of the musical The Wiz from 1974 to 1979.

Stephanie Mills has a lot of studio albums. They include,

  • Movin’ in the Right Direction (1974)
  • For the First Time (1975)
  • Love Has Lifted Me (1976)
  • What Cha’ Gonna Do with My Lovin’ (1979)
  • Tantalizingly Hot (1982)
  • Merciless (1983)
  • Something Real (1992)
  • Personal Inspirations (1994)
  • Born for This! (2004)

Who Is Stephanie Mills’ Husband?
