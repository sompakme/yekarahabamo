Is It Cake Season 2 Elimination Order and Judges

Is It Cake season 2 elimination order started with Jarid Altmark in week 1. Is It Cake Season 2 winner is Elizabeth Rowe.

Ten of the best hyper-realistic cake artist in the world joined the show. Their goal is to fool the judges into taking their share of the price pot of $120,000.

Mikey Day is the host, and three guest judges join to find cakes at the end of the episode. They will decide the winner and the baker that is eliminated from the competition each week.

The baker’s skills could win them over $100,000. In each episode, the bakers win $5000, and the winner heads to the round of Cake or Cash, where they get another $5000 if their guess comes true.

If the contestant fails to discover the cake, the money goes to the final pot, and the prize money on Cake or Cash gets increased.

The last baker standing will get crowned the Is It Cake? champion.

The season 2 premiered on June 30, 2023, on Netflix.

Is It Cake Season 2 Elimination Order

Is It Cake season 2 elimination order began with Jarid Altmark. Spirit Wallace was the last to be eliminated on the show.

The bakers were eliminated each week based on their performance along with evaluation from the judges. The following is the order of elimination for Is It Cake Season 2:

  • Episode 1: Jarid Altmark
  • Epsiode 2: Justin Salinas
  • Episode 3: Liz Marek
  • Episode 4: Corterrius Allen
  • Episode 5: No One
  • Episode 6: Kayla Giddings
  • Episode 7: Pete Tidwell and Danya Smith
  • Episode 8: Spirit Wallace and Miko Kaw Hok Uy
  • Winner: Elizabeth Rowe

Who Won Is It Cake Season 2?

Elizabeth Rowe won Is It Cake season 2. In the final round, judges are unable to recognize the edible tool bag with tools.

In the episode, she created a toolbag with tools.

According to the guest judges Catta-Preta, Chris, and Taylor, the detailing on the bag handles and stitching on the leather was incredible.

They were also impressed with the speckled paint on the box cutter.

The judge’s debated between Miko Kaw Hok UY Mona Lisa painting and Elizabeth tool bag.

At last, Elizabeth won the title of Is It Cake season 2 winner and received the grand prize of $75,000.

Besides winning the grand prize, she earned $15,000 by winning three episodes.

The winner of Is It Cake season 1, Andrew Fuller, toook $50,000 home.

In Is It Cake season 2, winner Elizabeth Rowe took $90,000. She decided to repair her ceiling and floor from the flooding with the money. She also mentioned she would use some money for her wedding.

In recent days, Rowe has been handling her bakehouse, The London Baker.

She added on Instagram being on the 2nd season of Is It Cake was a memorable experience for so many reasons.

At age 21, she started making cakes without professional training or schooling.

Meet Is It Cake Season 2 Judges

Is It Cake season 2 judges include Chrishell Stause, Joel McHale, Taylor Tomlinson, and more.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 1 Judges

Is It Cake season 2 episode 1 judges are Chrishell Stause, Blake Anderson, and Max Jobrani.

In episode 1, Everything Is Cake!, among ten bakers, five cake artists compete for up to $10,000 within 10 hours.

The first five bakers to find a cake in the living room compete in the episode, and Liz, Danya, Miko, Corterrius, and Jarid discover the cake.

The rest of them headed over to the gallery, and they baked on the next episode.

They make the cake according to their desire and which is personal to them.

Among Danya and Liz, the judges announced the winner, Sugar Creek Show owner Liz Marek. She won $5000 and moved to the Cake or Cash?

At last, Jarid Altmark from Orlando, FL, was eliminated from the competition.

Liz played for another $5000 and won it after guessing the right safe.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 2 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 2 judges are Nico Santo, Joel McHale, and Ally Love.

In episode 2, Body By Cake, the bakers saw six items in a gym where five of them were real while one was cake.

Each chose a gym item that they would make into a cake.

The contestant got 8 hours to complete their cake, and they created the following gym items:

  • Spirit: Kettlebells
  • Kayla: Bosu Ball
  • Pete: Boxing Gloves
  • Elizabeth: Yoga Mat & Blocks
  • Justin: Duffle Bag & Water Bottle

The judges selected Pete Tidwell with Boxing Gloves as the winner of episode 2, Body By Cake.

Justin Salinas from Bakersfield, California, was eliminated from the competition in 2nd week.

Pete played for another $5000, and among two protein powder containers, he spotted the cake and won $10,000 in episode 2.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 3 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 3 judges are Chloe Fineman, Kirby Howell-Baptiste, and Chris Witaske.

In episode 3, Cake University, the bakers had to find one cake hidden in the college bedroom, and only four of them could compete in this round while others remained in the next episode.

Liz Marek from Beaverton, OR, was the only baker who couldn’t fool the judges, and she got eliminated in the 3rd episode.

Meanwhile, Judges loved Danya Smith’s cake, and she won $5000.

She played to win another $5000, where she had to discover a backpack cake. She chose the right backpack and won another $5000.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 4 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 4 judges are Anna Camp, Melissa Villasenor, and Joel Kim Booster.

In episode 4, That 90’s Cake, the contestant had to discover one cake from the seven items found in the ’90s.

Miko, Corterrius, Elizabeth, and Pete competed in the competition choosing the 90’s items.

The judges chose Elizabeth as the winner of episode 4, looking at the visual detail, and won $5000.

Unfortunately, Corterrius Allen from Shelby, MS, got eliminated from the competition.

On the Cake and Cash round, the host showed Two Fanny Pack, and Elizabeth could not find the cake, so she only took $5000 in the round, while the amount went to the final pot.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 5 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 5 judges are Brandon Kyle Goodman, Cristela Alonzo, and Kate Flannery

In episode 5, ‘S’more Cake Please,’ the cash prize was raised to $15,000.

Spirit, Danya, and Elizabeth found the lantern at the campsite and competed for up to $15,000.

As the judges could not find any of the baker’s cake, no one went home in episode 5.

The judges chose Elizabeth as the winner of 5th episode, and she was up for the Cake or Cash round.

She again could not guess the cake and ended with only $5000. So, the $10,000 went to the final pot.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 6 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 6 judges Charli, Heidi, and Dixie D’ Amelio.

In episode 6, ‘So Fresh and So Cake,’ the winner could walk away with up to $20,000.

Miko, Pete, and Kayla competed in the round creating the cake with bathroom items.

Kayla Giddings from Lafayette, LA, could not fool the judges, Dixie D’ Amelio, Charli, and Heidi, so she got eliminated from the contest in episode 6.

Miko won the $5000 and moved on to Cake or Cash round for another $15,000.

However, he could not guess between the two laundry hampers, and the money went to the final pot.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 7 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 7 judges are Jeff Dye, Chiney Ogwumike, and Flula Borg.

There are three spots in the finale, which mean no matter what, two bakers are eliminated from the show.

In the semifinal episode 7, Cake Me Out To The Ballgame; five bakers competed for up to $20,000.

Each chose a sports item that would replicate into a cake to get into the finale.

Again Elizabeth won the round and headed for another $15,000.

Pete Tidwell and Danya Smith were eliminated from episode 7.

In the Cake or Cash round, she selected the wrong coolers.

Is It Cake Season 2 Episode 8 Judges

Is It Cake Season 2 episode 8 judges are Catta-Preta, Chris Redd, and Taylor Tomlinson.

In episode 8, Winner Cakes All!, the contestant got a baking assistant to make a cake worth $75,000.

Spirit, Elizabeth, and Miko select their assistant, Liz, Pete, and Danya, respectively.

After ten hours, the judges Catta-Preta, Chris, and Taylor Tomlinson could recognize the cake of Spirit Wallace, so she went home.

After debating over the two cakes of Miko and Elizabeth, the judges chose Elizabeth Rowe, and she won $75,000.
