Getting Started In The Industry

Before Dylan began his acting career, he has already produced, directed and starred in numerous short comedy films which was released through his personal YouTube account. During that time, he was also involved in a band called Slow Kids at Play as a drummer. He had his first ever acting break in TV when he landed a role as one of the main casts in MTV’s Teen Wolf which is a series based on the 1985 film of the same title. He initially was supposed to play the character of Scott, however when he read the script he decided he wants to audition for the part of Stiles instead. After about four auditions, he was cast as Stiles and started playing the role in 2010.

A year after, he was introduced to the movie audience through his film debut starring in the independent comedy film High Road. After two years, he then took a hiatus from filming Teen Wolf as he was about to film two movies namely The Internship and The Maze Runner in 2013. This was the biggest break he’s had in the movie industry where he won numerous awards.
