Jason Aldean's Wife Brittany Kerr Still in Hospital After Daughter's Birth

On the mend! Jason Aldean and his wife, Brittany Kerr, welcomed their daughter on Monday, February 4, but the former cheerleader revealed on her Instagram Story that they are still at the hospital.

“When your labor goes amazing … but then things go south,” the mother of two, 30, captioned footage of her wiggling feet. “Still hospital living with my trooper of a hubs and a precious little girl. One blood transfusion … and swollen everything.”

She added in a second video: “We’ve been in the hospital for days now, and I’m ready to take these fat little piggies home.” The American Idol alum giggled, and her husband, 41, could be heard laughing in the background.

This isn’t the first time Kerr and the country crooner have given fans a glimpse of their hospital stay. On Wednesday, February 6, the North Carolina native posted a video to Instagram of their 14-month-old son, Memphis, meeting his little sister, Navy, for the first time.

The couple’s baby boy wore a “Big Brother” shirt he rested his cheek against the infant’s face. “See Navy? You give her sugar?” the musician asked as he introduced the young siblings.
