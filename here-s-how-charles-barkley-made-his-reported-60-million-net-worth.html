Here's How Charles Barkley Made His Reported $60 Million Net Worth

Highlights

  • Barkley's NBA career and endorsements allowed him to accumulate a massive $60 million net worth.
  • Barkley's transition from the NBA to being an analyst on Inside the NBA brought him fame, financial gain, and endorsement deals.
  • Barkley's various endorsement deals, including with Nike, McDonald's, Capitol One, T-Mobile, and Weight Watchers, have contributed significantly to his overall wealth.

Charles Barkley made the bulk of his $60 million net worth during his NBA career. However, Barkley has ventured out since then, delving into various different avenues to diversify his interests that have made him a lot of money.

Barkley, nicknamed "Chuck," "Sir Charles," and "The Round Mound of Rebound" during his NBA days was a ferocious power forward that revolutionized the position. Though he was undersized by the standards of the day, Barkley's skills paved the way for smaller power forwards in today's game. Barkley has made a great career for himself and has made a lot of money along the way.

Barkley became an All-Star caliber player during his time with the NBA. He was also selected to the All-NBA First Team five times. Barkley's performance on the court was rewarded with big contracts. Barkley, however, made a big chunk of his money from commercials and endorsements for various brand names. Those endorsements and commercials allowed Barkley to transition seamlessly from the NBA to being an analyst on the Emmy Award-winning show, Inside the NBA.

On Inside the NBA, Barkley became a household name and a recognizable face on television. His unfiltered style of commentary often brings humor and some controversy. Barkley's 24 years on Inside the NBA have brought him fame, financial gain, and a slew of endorsement deals and small acting roles throughout his career as an analyst. All of which are just a small insight as to how Charles Barkley has made his massive $60 million net worth.

14 Charles Barkley Begins NBA Career With The Philadelphia 76ers

Charles Barkley made $9 million with the Philadelphia 76ers

  • Barkley's NBA career started with the 76ers

Barkley was drafted by the Philadelphia 76ers in 1984. He played 6 seasons with the 76ers. He quickly became one of their key contributors alongside NBA legend Moses Malone.

During his six seasons with the 76ers, Barkley amassed over $9 million. He represented the 76ers six times at the All-Star game.

13 Charles Barkley Signs With The Phoenix Suns

Charles Barkley made over $14 million with the Phoenix Suns

  • Barkley played with the Suns for four years

Barkley was traded to the Phoenix Suns in the 1992-1993 NBA season. He played in 4 seasons for the Suns and it proved to be his most successful years as a player and financially.

In his 4 years with the Suns Barkley made over $14 million and was voted as the league MVP in 1993. He even led the Suns to the NBA finals in 1993 but they were beaten by Michael Jordan's Chicago Bulls.

12 Charles Barkley Begins Endorsement Deals With Right Guard Commercial

Charles Barkley made between $5 million and $10 million over the course of his career

  • Barkley's sense of humor was displayed in the Right Guard commercial which became what he was known for when endorsing products

Sir Charles is not only known for being a ferocious competitor on the court, but he is also known for his humor and wit.

During his playing days, he teamed up with numerous brand names. His commercial with Right Guard has proven to be one of his more humorous ones. It has Barkley riding on a horse leading a hunting party to hunt for foxes while trying to sound affluent and posh.

11 Charles Barkley Becomes Video Game Royalty

Charles Barkley was paid an undisclosed amount of money for Shut Up and Jam

  • Barkley's video game was very popular upon its release

During his MVP season in 1993, Barkley started to become a sports household name. Corporate America began to take notice via the sport video game market and approached Barkley with a deal.

Sega Genesis developed Charles Barkley's Shut Up and Jam. It brought Barkley an undisclosed payday and showed the popularity he garnered during that time.

10 Charles Barkley Ends Lucrative NBA Career With The Houston Rockets

Charles Barkley made $17 million while playing for the Houston Rockets

  • Barkley spent his last four years with the NBA with the Rockets

Barkley's last four years in the NBA were played with the Houston Rockets. He teamed up with fellow MVP Hakeem Olajuwon in an attempt to win an NBA title. It proved to be his least productive years in the NBA.

Barkley suffered various injuries over his NBA career and a decrease in his skills. However, Barkley still made close to $17 million in his four years with the Rockets.
