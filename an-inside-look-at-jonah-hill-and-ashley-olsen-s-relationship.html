An Inside Look At Jonah Hill And Ashley Olsen's Relationship

We're obsessed with Hollywood friendships. This is especially true of the ones that seem so outlandish or somewhat unlikely.

For example, did you have any idea that Kristen Stewart is friendly with Kanye West? What about Justin Bieber and Jennifer Aniston? Or Howard Stern's relationship with his former enemy, Rosie O'Donnell?

Everyone tends to know about the close relationship between Jonah Hill and Leonardo DiCaprio. However, that's due to all of the press they did when promoting Martin Scorsese's The Wolf of Wallstreet. But did you know about Jonah Hill's friendship with Ashley Olsen?

Yes... THAT Ashley Olsen.

Ashley, of Mary-Kate and Ashley, Olsen.

Jonah Was First Spotted With Mary-Kate, Not Ashley

According to W Magazine, the world first found out about Jonah's bond with the Olsen Twins back in 2015, when Mary-Kate and her then-husband Olivier Sarkozy were seen hugging Jonah in New York City. A couple of years later, Ashley was seen doing the same. And it's usually her that Jonah is spotted with.

While celebrity friendships are usually not worth hiding, Jonah has certainly kept his relationship with Ashley Olsen on the down-low, along with an assortment of other revealing facts about his life.

Their Friendship Began When They Were Coming Up In Hollywood Together

The origin of Jonah's relationship with both Ashley and Mary-Kate isn't all that surprising. They were all famous young people working in the industry in Los Angeles during the exact same time.

Up-and-coming actors tend to gravitate toward one another since they're all going through an extremely unique situation. Although to be fair, Ashley and Mary-Kate were hugely successful at that time. While the majority of their Hollywood success came when they were child-stars in their various movies and on Full House, The Olsens also had television shows when they were teenagers. Jonah, on the other hand, was just starting out. He didn't get his breakthrough roles until 2007's Knocked Up and Superbad.

Jonah's Style Game Has Been Upped By Ashley

Since their days in Hollywood, Jonah has been seen hanging out with Ashley in New York. Given Ashley's influence in the fashion industry, Jonah seems to step-up his outfit game whenever he's with her.

All of this appears to shock the paparazzi who think that the famous pair are mismatched. Part of that may have to do with the fact that Ashley does her best to stay away from social media and the limelight. She's intensely private and therefore doesn't want to be spotted with anyone from her personal life, according to The Daily Mail.

Jonah, on the other hand, isn't afraid of the press. He's almost always out and about with his celebrity pals, whether he's at dinner with Channing Tatum or watching a basketball game at Madison Square Garden with his high-school bro, Adam Levine.

Ashley Has Been There For Jonah During His Darkest Days

Most famously, Ashley and Jonah were spotted together during Easter 2018. While their stroll through New York's Lower East Side looked casual enough, it might have meant more.

Jonah's brother, Jordan Feldstein, passed away a few months prior due to a heart attack. Jonah, who was obviously distraught, had barely been seen since. Ashley's visit may have been about offering her condolences to him. Or, at the very least, she was there to give him some sort of sense of normalcy.

Jonah Couldn't Let Winning An Award Take Away From A Chance To Praise Ashley

But Ashley is also there to support Jonah during the good times. According to People, she and her sister, Mary-Kate, attended The Wall Street Journal's 2018 Innovator Awards where Jonah was being honored with the film award. The event was held at the Museum of Modern Art right in the heart of Manhattan, New York. Ashley and Mary-Kate even sat right next to Jonah at his table when he received the prestigious honor.

In all likelihood, he had invited them to share in this joyous occasion. This is a huge deal for the twins, as they rarely appear at the same venue in order to break away from the "Olsen Twin" image that plagued their childhood. So, it tells us just how important Jonah is to them.

This respect is clearly reciprocated as Jonah made the point to honor the Olsen Twins in his Innovator Awards acceptance speech. He claimed that he has been inspired by them since they were all kids together in Hollywood.

Their Connection Remains Shrouded In Mystery

While some of Jonah's other celebrity friendships are highly publicized, it's nice that he has one that we know barely anything about. It tells us that there's some true authenticity to it. That it means something to him beyond the perks of publicity.

And the same goes for Ashley, who has remained out of the public eye for years. While she could easily be ridding Jonah's growing coattails, she chooses to keep her platonic love for him between them. That must mean the world to him given how likely it is that people attempt to use his fame for their own selfish desires.

At the end of the day, Jonah Hill and Ashley Olsen's friendship is still very much a mystery and that's exactly how they'd like to keep it.
