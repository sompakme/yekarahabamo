Beau Clark News - Us Weekly

Name:Beau Clark

About

Born in Fort Lauderdale, Florida on March 28, 1980, Beau Clark spent most of his formative years in Italy before moving back to America with his mother. where the two relocated to Nashville, Tennessee together.

In high school, Clark was passionate about photography and won several local awards for his work, which led to him enrolling in a summer acting program in New York City.

When he first moved to Los Angeles after college, Clark landed multiple acting jobs, including a part in 2003’s Darkwolf and a role in the 2010 short film The Loneliness of the Long Distance Dreamer He was also featured in the Lifehouse music video “You and Me.”

He currently works in Los Angeles as a commercial casting director.

Clark began dating Stassi Schroeder in February 2018 and the two made their first public appearance in June of that same year. The Vanderpump Rules star explained at the time that the couple loved to play video games, take their dogs on walks, get brunch and meet up with their friends during their time together.

In October 2018, Clark officially joined the cast of Vanderpump Rules, where his relationship with Schroeder relationship hit a few bumps in the road before the two eventually got engaged in June 2019.

The pair were set to tie the not in October 2020 but were forced to reevaluate due to the pandemic. They went on to wed in a secret ceremony in April 2020 and headed to Europe in May 2022 for a second wedding with their friends and family.

In January 2021, the couple announced the birth of their first child together, daughter Hartford Charlie Rose Clark.
