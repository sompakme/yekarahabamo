The Rock On What Hes The Best At: Horizontal Hula

The Rock says he’s the best at laying the SmackDown, just not in the way one might expect.

Speaking on The Pivot Podcast, “The Brahma Bull” was asked to describe what he’s the best at.

“Horizontal hula,” The Rock said with a laugh.

The former WWE Champion was asked about his game plan, and the hosts compared it to a movie script.

“Yeah, you got a beginning and an end. If you’re good, you get two [climaxes],” he said while everyone laughed along.

When again asked what he’s the best at, The Rock playfully noted that, besides the horizontal hula, he excels with things he’s passionate about.

“I think I’m pretty good at a lot of things. In terms of being the best at something, besides, egotistically, horizontal hula, I’m pretty good at what I love to do, and things that I love to do, I’m passionate about.”

In addition to his successful acting career, The Rock continues to build his business profile; he is an owner of the XFL, and he is also the founder of Teremana Tequila.
