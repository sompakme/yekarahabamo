Christina Milian (Christine Flores) is an American RnB singer with a net worth of $4.5 million. In addition to being a singer, Christina is also a songwriter, dancer, producer and actress.

Born on September 26, 1981 in New Jersey, Christina started her music career at the age of 19 when she agreed to a contract with recording company Def Jam. The following year she released her debut album which contained hits like “When You Look at Me” and “AM to PM”, with the later charted among the Top 40 on the US Billboard Top 100.

Christina has over four studio albums to her name to date which were released under different labels including Island Records, Young Money and Intersecope Records. She has played a main role in several films including “Love don’t Cost a Thing”, “Be Cool”, “Pulse”, Man of The House” and “Christmas Cupid”.

Christina has been nominated for two Grammy Awards and won several awards including The Reign Award for Outstanding Achievement and Ozone Award for Best R&B Female Artist.
