The 'What Men Want' Cast: Who Has The Highest Net Worth?

What Men Want came out in 2019, and while it received mixed reviews, most people will agree that the cast's acting was a pleasure to watch. Particularly Taraji P. Henson, who was the lead, was widely praised for her performance. The movie was loosely based on What Women Want, the 2000 Mel Gibson movie.

Most of the actors had a lot of experience by the time they were cast in the movie, and as such, many of them had already built a considerable fortune. But who is the richest cast member? Let's find that out.

10 Max Greenfield - $3 Million

Starting this list is Max Greenfield, with a $3 million net worth. This actor has built his fortune with his appearances in hugely successful movies and series, such as Veronica Mars, Modern Men, New Girl, and many more. He also had a brief role in Gilmore Girls. In addition, he joined the cast of American Horror Story in the series' fifth season.

9 Tamala Jones - $4 Million

Tamala Jones' first acting role was a guest appearance in the sitcom California Dreams. After that, her career started ramping up. She co-starred in For Your Love and The Tracy Morgan Show, and later on she had recurring roles in The Parent 'Hood and Veronica's Closet. Some of the movies she's worked on include Booty Call and The Wood. She's now worth $4 million.

8 Wendi McLendon-Covey - $5 Million

Wendi McLendon-Covey had quite an impressive career before What Men Want. She started acting in college, and after graduating, she joined a popular improvisational comedy group in Los Angeles called The Groundlings.

Her breakthrough was the movie Bridesmaids in 2011, and after that, she appeared on projects like What to Expect When You're Expecting, The Single Moms Club, Blended, Think Like a Man Too, and many more. She currently has a $5 million net worth.

7 Josh Brener - $5 Million

Josh Brener is currently worth $5 million, and his fortune comes mainly from his acting jobs and his work as a voiceover actor. Apart from What Men Want, he appeared on a few episodes of House of Lies and The Big Bang Theory. He also voiced Neeku Vozo in Star Wars Resistance, Donatello in Rise of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and Dylan Dalmatian in 101 Dalmatian Street.

6 Kellan Lutz - $5 Million

Some of the readers might know Kellan Lutz for his role as Emmett Cullen in The Twilight Saga. The staggering success of the movie adaptations of those books certainly contributed greatly to the actor's $5 million net worth. He also voiced the title character in the movie Tarzan, played Hercules in the 2014 movie The Legend of Hercules, and currently stars in FBI: Most Wanted. Kellan has a separate career as a model, and was featured in the 2010 Calvin Klein X underwear campaign.

5 Pete Davidson - $8 Million

Pete Davidson is mostly known for his amazing work on Saturday Night Live and for his high-profile engagement to Ariana Grande. He is one of the youngest cast members in the history of SNL, being only 20 years old when he was selected, and his career has done nothing but grow since then.

He filmed a comedy special in 2016, and in 2019, he did a short series of comedy shows with John Mulaney, called Sundays with Pete & John. His current reported net worth is $8 million.

4 Aldis Hodge - $10 Million

Aldis Hodge had a tough life growing up, but with hard work and talent, he managed to build his $10 million fortune. He started his acting career in the '90s, when he was just a kid, appearing in movies like Die Hard with a Vengeance and Bed of Roses. He then went on to work on Straight Outta Compton, Leverage, and also voiced King, Wrecka in 52 episodes of the series A.T.O.M.

3 Erykah Badu - $10 Million

Erykah Badu made most of her $10 million net worth with her work as a singer and songwriter. She has built an incredibly impressive career for herself, starting with her debut album, Baduizm, which blew everyone away with its almost instant success. The album was certified triple platinum. Since then, she has been releasing amazing music, while also dabbling into acting occasionally.

2 Taraji P. Henson - $25 Million

The star of What Men Want has amassed a $25 million fortune, and it's not really surprising when reviewing her resume. Taraji P. Henson's breakthrough was in the movie Baby Boy, alongside Tyrese Gibson, in 2001. She was nominated for an Oscar for her work in The Curious Case of Benjamin Button, and she has starred in several films made by her good friend Tyler Perry, including The Family That Preys and I Can Do Bad All By Myself.

1 Tracy Morgan - $70 Million

At the top of the list is Tracy Morgan, with a $70 million net worth. He began his career as a Saturday Night Live cast member in the '90s, and later on, he starred in his colleague Tina Fey's sitcom 30 Rock, earning an Emmy Award. He's currently the star of The Last O.G., playing Tray Barker. Some of his most popular work includes First Sunday, Adam Sandler's The Longest Yard, and his comedy specials, Tracy Morgan: Bona Fide and Tracy Morgan: Staying Alive.
