Kris Statlander vs. Diamante Announced, Updated Card For 11/25 AEW Rampage

Kris Statlander will try to get back to her winning ways tonight on AEW Rampage.

After losing her TBS Championship to Julia Hart at Full Gear, Kris Statlander returns to action later tonight on a special Saturday episode of Rampage to take on Diamante.

This is in addition to a huge match that All Elite Wrestling President Tony Khan announced on social media yesterday that will see Wheeler Yuta challenge Katsuyori Shibata for the Ring of Honor Pure Championship.

The updated card for tonight’s episode of AEW Rampage is as follows:

  • Ring of Honor Pure Championship: Katsuyori Shibata (c) vs. Wheeler Yuta
  • Kris Statlander vs. Diamante

Rampage will be part of a special three hour block of All Elite Wrestling programming programming as the show will air back to back with tonight’s episode of AEW Collision.

WrestleZone will have live coverage of tonight’s episode of AEW Rampage later this evening.
