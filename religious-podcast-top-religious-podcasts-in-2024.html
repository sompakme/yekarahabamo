The Happy Hour with Jamie Ivey is a podcast that offers engaging and thought-provoking conversations centered around faith. Hosted by Jamie Ivey, each episode features Jamie chatting with a different friend about “the big things in life, the little things in life, and everything in between.”

Listeners are treated to genuine and inspiring discussions that offer insights and inspiration for navigating life’s challenges through a faith-based lens.

With a diverse lineup of guests, The Happy Hour with Jamie Ivey offers a unique blend of perspectives and experiences. From authors and pastors to artists and entrepreneurs, each episode invites listeners to connect with others and find encouragement in their own faith journey.

Whether you’re a Christian woman seeking practical advice, spiritual inspiration, or a sense of community, The Christian Girl’s Tea Podcast is an excellent resource. Tune in weekly to join Sarah Thompson and her guests as they delve into the topics that matter most to Christian women.
