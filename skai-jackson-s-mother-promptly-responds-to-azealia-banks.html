Skai Jackson's Mother Promptly Responds To Azealia Banks

Azealia Banks‘ Wednesday (May 11) is going from not so good, to kind of bad. Scheduled as headliner for London’s “Born and Bred” festival, the “The Big Beat” artist was removed as headliner for her racist remarks towards Zayn Malik on Twitter (May 10).

However, it was the 24-year-olds feud with 14-year-old Skai Jackson that caused the Internet to erupt Tuesday night (May 11). Since her explosive back and forth, Jackson’s mother, Kiya Cole, responded and had several choice words for the “212” artist.

“She’s too old to go at it with a 14-year-old, but not too young for me to see her.” Cole wrote.
