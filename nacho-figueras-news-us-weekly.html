Nacho Figueras News - Us Weekly

Name:Nacho Figueras

Birthday:March 4, 1977

About

Born in 1977, this Argentine polo player is ranked one of the top 100 polo players in the world and is the face of Ralph Lauren’s Black Label line. Figueras is the captain of the Black Watch Polo team and models on the side. He has been playing polo since age 17 and has been asked to donate his polo equipment to the American Museum of Natural History because of his success.

Married to photographer Delfina Blaquier, Figueras splits his time between New York City, Palm Beach, Fla., and Argentina.
