What Max Kallschmidt Really Does For A Living Aside From Angering Fans Of Welcome To Plathville

Fans of Welcome To Plathville are all in agreement on one thing, and it's that Max Kallschmidt isn't a good guy. Even though the TLC star claims to feel like he is "Mr. Misunderstood," fans aren't willing to give him a second chance. This is thanks in large part to his breakup from fan-favorite Moriah Plath and the way he's handled himself since.

Max was first introduced on Welcome To Plathville fans in season 2 as Moriah's long-term boyfriend. Fans got front-seat views of the couple's transition from close friends to dating, and it really seemed like the two were head-over-heels in love with each other. Even Moriah's notoriously strict parents, Kim and Barry Plath, willingly and happily welcomed Max into the family.

In season 3, Max gifted Moriah a promise ring, promising to marry her one day. It seemed like everything was peachy for the couple, but sadly, it wasn't the case. Despite becoming an important member of the cast of the reality tv show, Max isn't going to be appearing on the show anytime soon.

While fans are certainly team Moriah, they still are curious about what Max has been up to post-Welcome to Plathville.

What Is Max Kallschmidt Doing Post Welcome To Plathville?

Max Kallschmidt made sure to get his side of the story out after not being featured in future seasons of Welcome to Plathville. The reality star made a Youtube page where he explained his view of what happened with Moriah and the cause of their breakup.

Fans weren't exactly thrilled with his response, with many of them actually becoming angry with Max. He didn't seem to have any sympathy for Moriah and was flippant during the discussion of his infidelity.

During the video, he said he felt a lot of pressure from Moriah to move in with her, Ethan, and Olivia after they moved from Cairo to Florida. He did claim that he and Moriah remained friends after the breakup, and while there was definitely some tension between them at first, it seems like he is still friendly with Moriah and her family.

On Instagram, he was even spotted wishing Moriah's younger sister, Lydia, happy birthday. Despite their parent's strict rules, most of the kids do have Instagram.

Max has moved on from his Welcome To Plathville days, with his Instagram account now dedicated to posting pictures and videos of the fun times he's having with friends and family. He's also been traveling a lot and most recently being spotted checking out San Francisco.

Fans on Reddit have long questioned Max's real reasoning for going on the show and dating Moriah. Some feel that he came on purely for fame and to promote his businesses, but considering his YouTube channel hasn't been updated since posting his video about the breakup, his intentions might have been purer than skeptics think. He appears to be single but hasn't commented on his relationship status.

Has Moriah Plath Moved On After Her Breakup With Max Kallschmidt?

Moriah hasn't moved on from her breakup with Max, but she doesn't seem to be as strung up on it as she once was. Given that Max was Moriah's first kiss and first boyfriend, chances are she's going to need some time to get over the hurt caused by the relationship ending.

We don't know what she did with the promise ring she received from Max either, but she's definitely not wearing it anymore. During season three, Moriah was often seen crying over the breakup and even took to writing some devastatingly sad songs about it.

But Moriah was much less held down by the breakup in season four, even taking time to go on a few dates. If anything, her breakup from Max helped Moriah understand what she's looking for in a man.

What Is Welcome To Plathville About?

The reality show Welcome to Plathville premiered on TLC in 2019 and follows the lives of the Plath family. The Plath's are a conservative Christian family who has chosen to live a simple life, with much of it dedicated to God.

They are situated in rural Georgia, USA, and live with limited exposure to modern technology, such as television and the internet. Many fans have questioned how real the show is over the years, but creators and the cast claim everything is true.

The family consists of parents Kim and Barry Plath and their nine children, some of whom are grown up and have moved out of the family home.

The show mainly focuses on the lives of the six youngest children (including Micah Plath), who are homeschooled and have had limited exposure to the outside world.

The show explores the family dynamics, including the relationships between the children and their parents, as well as the challenges the family faces as they navigate the outside world and try to maintain their beliefs and values. The show also follows the older Plath children as they navigate their own lives outside the family home.

Welcome to Plathville was developed by TLC to offer a glimpse into the life of a conservative, Christian family living in modern times. Fans have responded positively to the show, showing there's a real interest in seeing how these families navigate the challenges of modern society while trying to stay true to their beliefs and values.

The show has truly tested the family as well and eventually led to the divorce of Kim and Barry Plath, something that viewers of season one would have never seen coming.

Despite their devout Christian beliefs, the couple finally called it quits after years of bickering at the tale end of season four. Fans were scared that the show wouldn't be returning after Kim and Barry announced their divorce, but thankfully season five will cover the dissolution of their marriage.
