American actor Tom Skerritt has an estimated net worth of $8 million. He has appeared in over 40 films and 200 television episodes since 1962. He is notable for his performance in A River Runs Through It, Alien, MASH, Picket Fences, and Up In Smoke.

As an actor, he made his debut in the 1962 film War Hunt in which he played the role of Sgt. Stan Showalter. This was followed by his appearance on numerous TV shows and films.

His most recent film projects include Whiteout, For Sale By Owner, Rivers of a Lost Coast, Redemption Road, Ted, and Field of Lost Shoes. 

He has been a recipient of numerous award, including Emmy Awards, DVD Exclusive Awards, National Board of Review of Motion Pictures, Western Heritage Awards, and Screen Actors Guild Awards.
