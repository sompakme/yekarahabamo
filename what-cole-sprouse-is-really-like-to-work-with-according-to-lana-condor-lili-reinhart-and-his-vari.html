What Cole Sprouse Is Really Like To Work With, According To Lana Condor, Lili Reinhart, And His Vari

Cole Sprouse has been making headlines thanks to his now-notorious interview on the Call Her Daddy podcast, hosted by Alex Cooper. The actor gave Alex and her audience an uncensored view into his life, answering all of her questions with a lit cigarette in hand.

Despite the negative response from the public to the actor's persona, he has a pretty positive rep in Hollywood. Throughout his career, he's positively influenced many of his co-stars in positive ways and developed deep friendships, and even a meaningful romantic relationships, with them. Below, we shed some light on how Cole's various co-stars genuinely feel about the actor.

9 What Has Lana Condor Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Lana Condor and Cole Sprouse starred in Moonshot together in 2022. Their characters strike up a romantic connection despite their vast differences. Lana plays the uptight, type-A Sophie, and Cole stars as the much more easygoing Walt in the movie.

Lana has had only positive things to say about her time working with Cole on Moonshot. "Cole and I had the best time on set. He is a great friend and a really wonderful actor," said Lana to Insider. "It was my first movie out of To All the Boys, and it was really fun to kind of explore new territory."

Lana said she struggled to stop laughing during her takes because Cole was so funny on set. "He would make me laugh constantly. I wish I could be as good as all these other actors who never, ever break, but some things are just too funny," said Lana in an interview with Insider.

8 What Has Lili Reinhart Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Lili Reinhart and Cole Sprouse met on the set of Riverdale. Though it took some time for Lili to let her guard down, she and Cole struck up a serious relationship in 2017. They strived to keep their relationship out of the public eye, going out of their way to either deny or avoid questions about their relationship until 2018.

Fans thought the pair were in it for the long haul, so they were shocked to her rumors of their split in 2020. Cole confirmed their split in August 2020 via an Instagram post. "What an incredible experience I had, I'll always feel lucky and cherish that I had the chance to fall in love. I wish her nothing but the utmost love and happiness moving forward. All I'll say about it, anything else you hear doesn't matter," wrote Cole in the caption.

In an interview with Variety in May 2020, Lili spoke highly about working with Cole on Riverdale, despite their breakup. "Cole is one of my closest friends, and he's been a confidant for me on the show. We've been through a lot together over the past few years, and it's been really great to have him by my side. I feel lucky to have him as a friend and a co-star," said Lili.

7 What Has Dylan Sprouse Said About Working With His Brother, Cole Sprouse?

Dylan and Cole Sprouse built their careers on being "The Sprouse Twins." The brothers made an estimated $3.5 million during the three seasons they filmed The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. By the time their spin-off series, The Suite Life: On Deck, began filming, the boys were only 16 and already millionaires.

Despite their skyrocketing fame and net worth, the brothers maintained a close friendship while working together. In an interview with MTV in 2013, Dylan spoke about his experience working on The Suite Life with Cole, saying, "It was an awesome experience. We got to do it together, which is really rare, and we got to share that experience with each other." He went on to explain that they were able to help each other out on set and make the experience even more fun.

6 What Has KJ Apa Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

KJ Apa is another Riverdale co-star of Cole's. The two have been starring cast members since the show's first season. The pair have formed quite a bond throughout filming the series as well.

In a video interview with Harper's Bazaar in September 2018, KJ talked about his off-screen friendship with Cole. "We get along really well. We have a lot of fun on set. It's always nice when you can have a friendship with someone you're working with," said KJ.

5 What Has Brenda Song Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Brenda Song played the iconic London Tipton on The Suite Life of Zack and Cody and The Suite Life: On Deck. Not only did she become Disney Channel royalty thanks to the role, she had a front-row seat to Dylan and Cole growing up. "Cole and Dylan are like my little brothers. We grew up together. They're amazing, and I'm so proud of them and all their success," said Brenda to Entertainment Weekly in 2022.

She still keeps in contact with the boys as well. Brenda told People in March 2021 that she texts and talks with Cole and that she's super proud of him. "He's done such amazing work, and he's such a talent," said Brenda to People.

4 What Has Camila Mendes Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Camila Mendes plays Veronica Lodge on Riverdale and is also one of the show's original cast members. She and Cole have formed a friendship over their years on set, and she's been quoted multiple times complimenting his acting and professionalism on set "I feel like we've grown closer over the years. We have a really good working relationship, and it's always nice when you can also have a friendship with someone you work with," said Camila during a 2020 interview with Glamour Magazine.

3 What Has Debby Ryan Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Debby Ryan joined The Suite Life family in the spin-off series, The Suite Life: On Deck. She played Cole's love interest on the show, Bailey Pickett. During an interview with Hollywood Life in February 2011, Debby Ryan spoke highly about Cole Sprouse and their off-set friendship. "Cole and I are really close. We've been friends for a long time, and we have a lot of fun together. It's great to work with someone you're so comfortable with," said Debby.

The two have maintained a friendship was well, with Cole being spotted at Debby's birthday parties and even her engagement party in 2018.

2 What Has Ashley Tisdale Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Ashley Tisdale played Maddie on The Suite Life of Zack and Cody. Cole's character, Cody, had a massive crush on Maddie, which played out throughout the series featuring various funny storylines of Cody trying to court Maddie.

Ashley thoroughly enjoyed her time working with Cole and Dylan. "They're such a great duo. They're so much fun to work with, and they're such talented actors," said Ashley during a video interview with Young Hollywood in 2008.

Over ten years later, she confirmed to fans that she still feels that way. During a Twitter Q&A, the High School Musical star was asked about her favorite memory from working on The Suite Life of Zack and Cody set. "Definitely working with the Sprouse twins. We always had so much fun on set," said Ashley.

1 What Has Haley Lu Richardson Said About Working With Cole Sprouse?

Before she landed the part of the chronically ill-dressed Portia on The White Lotus, Haley Lu Richardson starred opposite Cole Sprouse in Five Feet Apart. During the press tour for the film, Haley sang Cole's praises and credited him with helping her get through filming the many emotionally brutal scenes in the movie.

"It was a very emotional movie, and Cole was always there for me. We would talk about the scenes and how we wanted to approach them, and we would check in with each other throughout the day," said Haley in a 2019 interview with The Hollywood Reporter. "We had a really strong connection and I think that really helped us in our performances."

During her 2019 Teen Vogue interview, she praised his dedication to the film and his ability to bring depth to the character. "Cole is a very deep thinker, and he brings a lot of complexity to the character. He's also very professional and always prepared. He really cared about making this film as authentic and honest as possible," said Haley.
