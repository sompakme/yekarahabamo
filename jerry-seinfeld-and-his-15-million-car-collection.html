Jerry Seinfeld and his $15 Million Car Collection

Jerry Seinfeld has one of the biggest Porsche collections in the world!

His collections are dominated by his 47 Porsche cars located in his $1.4 million garage. He owns an ultra-rare 959, a 1955 550 Spyder, Porsche Boxsters and the first 911 ever produced.  He also owns a Porsche Carrera GT superxar and Porsche Carrera GT.

Jerry has bought a silver Porsche 959 worth $700, 000. The car is a rare found, having only 337 copies worldwide.

His collection is estimated to be over $15 million.  He has a private mechanic who maintains quality standard of his garage, with the temperature monitored through a high-tech HVAC unit on the roof.

At Jerry's garage in Manhattan

Seinfeld, who has an estimated net worth of $800 million, is one of the top grossing comedianswho stars in the 1990s American comedy TV show “Seinfeld.” He earned $10 million with his Microsoft ads in 2008.  He is also a renowned author and speaker.  He currently stays on his 3-story compound on Central Park West which play host to his four garages, club room, kitchenette, elevator and pool table.

Jerry is married for 10 years to Jessica Sklar and they own a $50 million estate in East Hampton.

And just recently, Seinfeld is back with the second season of ‘Comedians in Cars Getting Coffee.’ The online series is about Seinfield having an auto trip and chat with the people who make him laugh. The first season featured Sarah Silverman and talk show host David Letterman. The latest episode features French comedian Gad Elmaleh, dubbed “the Seinfeld of France.”

Jerry and Gad rode in his 1950 Citroën 2CV while they talked about France and the US.
