Princess Beatrice of York has an estimated net worth of $5 million. She is the fifth,and the first female, in the line of succession to the thrones of 16 Commonwealth Realms.

Princess Beatrice Elizabeth Mary Windsor was born on August 8, 1988 in Portland Hospital, London. Officially known as Her Royal Highness, Princess Beatrice of York, she is the elder daughter of Prince Andrew, Duke of York and Sarah, Duchess of York.

Beatrice made the headlines in 2006 when it became known that the 17-year-old princess was dating a 24-year-old American who had pleaded guilty to assault and battery in the 2002 death of a fellow College of the Holy Cross student.

As a university student, Beatrice does not regularly carry out official royal duties; however, she does sometimes make appearances with the Royal Family at events, such as when she and her sister, Princess Eugenie, represented their father at a service of thanksgiving for her late aunt Diana, Princess of Wales, in 2007.
