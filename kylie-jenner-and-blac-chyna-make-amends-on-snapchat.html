Kylie Jenner And Blac Chyna Make Amends On Snapchat

In a twist of all twists, it looks like one strained relationship is back intact. Kylie Jenner and Blac Chyna took a photo earlier today (Apr. 21) on Snapchat which sent viewers up the wall with either joy or sheer indifference.
