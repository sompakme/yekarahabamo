Tom Cruise's New Friendship With Taylor Swift Reportedly Makes Her Friends And Family Uncomfortable

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift's friends and family are concerned about Cruise's intentions due to his involvement in Scientology.
  • Cruise reportedly believes befriending Swift is a strategic career move and is eager to collaborate on projects and introduce her to his London connections.
  • Tom Cruise's attendance at Taylor Swift's concert caused controversy as he missed his daughter's graduation.

A new friendship has apparently blossomed, but not everyone is happy about it. Tom Cruise was recently spotted having the time of his life at one of Taylor Swift's now iconic concerts at Wembley Stadium. He was very glad to have gotten the invite to the concert, but Taylor's circle is reportedly uncomfortable with him getting too close to the singer.

Sources are now claiming that Tom Cruise's involvement in Scientology is concerning to Taylor's friends and family, as they suspect he might have ulterior motives in befriending the superstar, but an insider assured that they have nothing to worry about.

Not Everyone Approves Of Tom Cruise’s New Friendship With Taylor Swift

Tom Cruise’s attendance at Taylor Swift’s Eras tour concert in London caused some controversy, especially because he missed his daughter’s graduation the day before. The actor is apparently looking forward to making friends with the singer, but not everyone is happy about this.

A source recently spoke about how important being invited to the Eras tour was for Tom Cruise, despite the fact that it caused concerns in Taylor’s circle.

“The invite to Taylor’s concert meant so much to Tom,” the insider explained. “He’s been floating ideas of how they can all collaborate on projects, and is also offering to introduce them to some of his favorite people and places in London.”

This eagerness is what’s reportedly making Taylor Swift’s friends uncomfortable, as they fear he’s trying to turn her into Scientology.

“As always with Tom, he’s very intense and excitable – he has this way of sweeping people up into his personal universe and not taking no for an answer,” the source admitted, but reaffirmed that the actor has no ulterior motives.

“Tom’s ties to Scientology are bound to make people in Taylor’s circle a little nervous. But, by all accounts, he’s got no agenda when it comes to religion anymore – he’s past all that.”

However, Tom Cruise might have a hidden agenda when it comes to his career, as he allegedly believes befriending Taylor is a good strategic move.

“He’s still personally devoted to Scientology, but his primary concerns these days, aside from his career, is expanding his social network in the UK and beyond.”

Tom Cruise Attending Taylor Swift's Concert Caused Controversy

It's not only people in Taylor Swift's circle who had a problem with Tom Cruise's closeness to the singer. When he was spotted at one of Taylor's shows at Wembley Stadium, a lot of fans pointed out that it was strange he had time to go to the concert but had missed his daughter Suri's graduation just the day before.

The actor is currently working in the UK, but people still pointed out that his absence was noticeable.
