Jennifer Lawrences Best Red Carpet Dresses: Pics

We’re taking a look back at Jennifer Lawrence‘s hottest red carpet style moments of all time! And there’s some seriously good ones.

After bursting onto the scene with the Oscar-nominated role in Winter’s Bone in 2010, the blonde beauty went on to become the highest-paid actress in the world in 2015 and 2016 after starring in blockbuster smashes like Hunger Games and X-Men. While promoting these huge hits, she wowed Us time and time again with her red carpet fashion sense. Landing atop countless best-dressed lists for her fab dress game, her looks have included everything from dramatic ballgowns (that pose as tripping hazards!) to short and sweet minis.
