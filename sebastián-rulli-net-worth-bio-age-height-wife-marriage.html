Career

Between 1995 and 1998, the actor dabbled in telenovelas in Argentina before settling in Mexico to pursue acting at the Centro de Educación Artstica de Televisa. In the year 2000, he made his cinematic debut in the telenovela Primer amor, a mil por hora, which was followed by Sin pecado concebido and Clase 406, both melodramas that established him as one of the most sought-after performers. In 2004, he co-starred in the telenovela Rub with actress Bárbara Mori, a role that catapulted him to worldwide fame.

Rulli continued to work in telenovelas such as Contra viento y marea, Mundo de fieras, and Pasión in the years that followed. Rulli has been in TV shows such as Mujer, casos de la vida real, Alegrijes y rebujos, Ugly Betty, and Amor mo, in addition to telenovelas. In 2008, he co-starred in the telenovela Un gancho al corazón, based on the Argentine telenovela Sos mi vida.

The next year, he featured in two episodes of the telenovela Cuando me enamoro, and starred in the telenovela Teresa, opposite Angelique Boyer, for which he got two Best Actor prizes from the New York Latin ACE Awards and the Bravo Awards.
In 2012, he co-starred in Nicandro Daz González’s telenovela Amores verdaderos, a remake of the Argentine telenovela Amor en custodia, which was created in 2005. He returned to collaborate with Angelique Boyer in the telenovela Lo que la vida me robó two years after Teresa.  This serial earned him Best Lead Actor honors at the 33rd TVyNovelas Awards.  Two years later, he returned to action in the telenovela Tres veces Ana, with Boyer, and won Best Actor at the TVyNovelas Awards.
