American real estate broker and businessman, Richard Howard "Rick" Hilton, has an estimated net worth of $300 million. He is the Chairman and co-founder of Hilton & Hyland, a real estate brokerages firm. He is a son of billionaire Barron Hilton and grandson of Conrad Hilton, founder of Hilton Hotels. He is also the father of socialites Paris Hilton and Nicky Hilton with his wife married actress Kathy Richards.

Richard Hilton earned his net worth through his residential and commercial investments in real estate, and his development of the gated community of Brentwood Estates. He co-brokered for the sale of Candy Spelling's Holmby Hills estate to Petra Ecclestone for $85 million. Representing both Spelling and Ecclestone, Hilton earned a reported $2 million commission. Hilton was born on 17 August 1955 in Los Angeles, California.
