Born Darius Earvin Johnson-Odom on September 28, 1989 in Raleigh, North Carolina, he attended Marquette College where he spent one season in junior college before moving to Hutchinson Community College where he earned first-team All-Big East as a freshman. He  averaged 21.6 points and 5.2 rebounds. In three seasons at Marquette, Johnson-Odom scored 1,649 points to rank 11th all-time in program history.

During the 2012 NBA Draft, Johnson-Odom was selected 55th overall by the Dallas Mavericks, but was immediately sold to the Los Angeles Lakers. During the 2012-2013 season, he was assigned in the D-League team, the Los Angeles D-Fenders, several times.
