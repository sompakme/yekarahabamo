New Becky Lynch T-Shirt Released On WWE Shop, Dolph Ziggler Set For MTV Host Role

New Becky Lynch T-Shirt Released On WWE Shop

One of the biggest indicators of a wrestler’s rise in popularity is often how well their merchandise sells – and as such, the release of a brand new Becky Lynch t-shirt can only be a good thing. Her ‘The Man’ design has drawn comparisons to the Austin 3:16 and nWo designs, mainly because of how simplistic it is. Many fans have already noted that they’ll be purchasing the shirt, and from now until WrestleMania and beyond, it’ll be interesting to see just how much more popular she gets.
