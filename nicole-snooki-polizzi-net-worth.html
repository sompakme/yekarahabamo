Chilean-American reality television personality, Nicole Elizabeth "Snooki" Polizzi has an estimated net worth of $5 million. Snooki gained great popularity as cast member on the MTV hit reality show Jersey Shore. She is also one of the highest paid reality star, currently earning $150,000 per episode. For the first season of Jersey Shore, Snooki earned $2,200 per episode. For season two her salary was increased to $30,000 per episode.

Polizzi was born in Santiago, Chile. Adopted when she was six months old, she was raised by Italian American parents. She received her nickname, Snooki, in middle school when her friends named her after "Snookie", a male character in Save the Last Dance, because she was the first of her friends to kiss a boy.
