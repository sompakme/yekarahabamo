Gavin Rossdale Returns to Twitter After Cheating Scandal: Photo

The @Seahawks win!

— GAVIN ROSSDALE (@GavinRossdale) December 21, 2015

And he’s back. Gavin Rossdale returned to Twitter on Sunday, December 20, after nearly a two-month break. The Bush frontman appeared to take a hiatus from the social media site amid his cheating scandal.

“The @Seahawks win!” Rossdale, 50, captioned a photo from the Seattle Seahawks vs. Cleveland Browns game. “Photo by @murphyjensen.”

The rocker appeared to watch the game from the stadium at CenturyLink Field in Seattle, Washington. He was joined by three pals: two male and one female.

Us Weekly broke the news last month that Rossdale had been cheating on his then-wife, Gwen Stefani, for more than three years with their nanny Mindy Mann. The No Doubt frontwoman, 46, found explicit text messages and nude photos shared between the two.

“The iPad was linked to Gavin’s phone,” a family source exclusively told Us. “One of the other nannies discovered the exchange and told Gwen.”

An insider added, “This was done right under Gwen’s nose.”
